Redesign NEU web calendar (Wireframe)

  • Type: Individual project (for Usibility of Human Interaction project)
  • My Role: Ideation, wireframes
  • Method: desktop wireframes
  • Tool: | Balsamiq |
  • Timeline: OCT 2016 (2 Weeks)

Process Overview

A common complaint of students is that while there are many activities going on at Northeastern each day, it’s difficult to find timely information about campus events. I was tasked with designing an improved version of this calendar application to better serve student’s needs.

The Problem

Due to the fact that students has diversity requirement on events' searching, i focus on how to let them search the event more effieciently in their ideal method on website.

  • I classified their needs in following types:
  • Search Event by Subject:The students who want to take events that in specific subject.
  • Search Event by Date:The students who have free time and want to join an events today or in specific days, no matter what kind of event it is.
  • Search Event by Feature Event: The events that univesity is highly recommended.
  • Search Event by Address: The students who have free time and want to join the events close to her location. >> Stress out Location
