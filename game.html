[CODE:GARBO] Narrative Game Design

  • Type: Team Project (for Digital Stroytelling project)
  • My Role: Interface Design,Artwork of Game, Story structure and Mapping
  • Method: Brainstorming >> Storytelling and Mapping >> Artwork and Prototype >> Players Testing
  • Tool: Painting on Ipad | Photoshop |inkdML
  • Timeline: May 2017 -- June 2017

Process Overview

The Garbo was a Spanish citizen who deliberately became a double agent against Nazi Germany during World War II.
We inspired by his story and made a narrative game.

The Problem

Initially we felt challenged by the non-fiction storytelling style. We had several brain storming sessions to decide what topics to choose from.
Therefore, we create a node map to identify players' choice. We organized two main branches with different results, and bulid the connection between two branches to lead different result.


Node Map

Artwork and Prototype

According to the node map, i painted all plots on Ipad, which accounts more than sixty pictures. I used the tone of brown as background to fit for the war situation. I used more aggresive color such as red and orange to stress out the thrilling and dangerous experience to be a double agent.


Unexpected Problems After Players Testing

  • There are cognitive gaps between the storytellers (us as a group) and the players
    • Since we have read extensive materials about Garbo. We assumed a lot of facts should be known by the players.
    -->>--We went back and edited the text to give players more background information,and i try to make more interface that connected by pictures not by node.
  • Our narrative story originally stays very true to facts. However factual the story was, it lacks creativity.
    -->>--we added more diversity, more options at each story line. We also created a series of rising actions leading to the climax.
