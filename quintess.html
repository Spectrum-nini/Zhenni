Process Overview

Whitle doing an internship in Quintess Real Estate, I design the brochure for open house and are designing a website as my side project, which will be later released as agent' new website. I am the only desinger and developer for this project.I handled all design product from user experience design and development.

The Problem

  • The brochure:

    what informations about house is the buyers'main concern?
    How to effectively organize it with fewer pages(less cost)?

  • The website:

    How to attract vistors contact us?(make website interface more attractive)


Branding and Brochure

The Intial Design of Agent

  • The font and color is too modern to fit for brochure, which many of houses listed on is Colonial style.
  • Home buying process is complex with unexpected troubles, so the house is unstable might reminds customers with the sense of no-confidence.

My Design of Agent

    After discussion with founder, we make the changes as following:
  • The classical red is trustful and formal. For example, many prestigious university use this style as introductory guide.
  • I changed a largerhouse to look it on ground indeed, and change words with elegant font style.
